## Step 4 — Term-level Metrics

Aggregated over the **set of unique terms** across all CQs, ignoring CQ id. This answers the coarse question: _does pred know the right concepts for this domain?_

Per-CQ accuracy (_does pred mention the right concepts in the right CQ?_) is reported separately in Step 5. The two metrics can diverge: an LLM with strong vocabulary but weak CQ-specific reasoning will score higher here than on per-CQ coverage.

- TP = gold term has an aligned pred term that appears anywhere in pred
- FN = gold term not covered by any pred term
- FP = pred term doesn't translate back to any gold term

## Step 5 — CQ Coverage

For each gold CQ, coverage is computed over its gold terms (classes and properties). A term is counted as covered only when its aligned pred term appears in the pred CQ with the same CQ id. This enforces CQ-local correctness.
