CQ4OE leaderboard for LLM-based ontology generation from competency questions. Runs are grouped by evaluation configuration so only identical settings are ranked against each other.

Choose an evaluation task

CQ4OE has two complementary tasks. Pick the one that matches what your method produces.

CQ2Term
Term-level recovery. Given a CQ, predict the required classes and properties. Single evaluation target over 99 CQs.
CQ2Onto

CQ2Onto evaluation targets

Five evaluation targets at different modeling depths. Pick one to view its leaderboard.

Term Recovery
Class and property alignment between predicted and gold ontology.
Property Characteristics
OWL flags such as functional, transitive, symmetric.
Triple (Domain and Range)
Whether each property has the right domain and range.
TBox Axioms
Strict structural matching of TBox axioms after term alignment.
Hierarchy Closure
Reasoner-based recovery of subsumptions (entailed, not just asserted).
